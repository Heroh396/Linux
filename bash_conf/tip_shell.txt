- Move:
	+ Ctrl + A	: head
	+ Ctrl + E	: tail

- Show dir:
	+ ls | grep folder_name
	+ history | grep folder_name

- Delete screen:	<Ctrl> + L
- Exit session:		<Ctrl> + D
- Find previous command:	<Ctrl> + R

- Font: https://github.com/powerline/powerline

- Convert JPG to PNG:
	mogrify -format png /path/to/*.jpg

- Resize scale image:
	mogrify -resize 50% *.png	or 320x240, x240
